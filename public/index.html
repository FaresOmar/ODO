About us

“Good afternoon, i am asking for a BMW 316i f30 water pump, is it available?” This is you. “Yes it is available the original one for 3000 EGP.” And this is the seller at the store. The above conversation takes place in the buying process everyone of us lives when it comes to buying a part for his/her car. This process could consume too much time or a little time. It could exhaust you or could be a satisfying one. It could be as it is or more complicated or more simpler. Odomotive aims to solve the problems you face when buying a part for your car. It is an online platform to search for, compare & buy different types of automotive aftersales products or services for different types and manufacturers of cars. Odomotive has tied-up with the leading distributors and suppliers of automotive parts to offer the largest variety of products at the best possible price. It aims to become the largest online marketplace of automotive spare parts & services in Egypt.
